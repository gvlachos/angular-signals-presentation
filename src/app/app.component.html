Angular Signals - A story of change

Angular signals were introduced with Angular 16 (2023) as part of a number of changes in Angular, also called the Angular Renaissance.
Used to synchronize application data with the UI (aka change detection) they intend to simplify developer experience and provide the foundations for future improvements in Angular.
